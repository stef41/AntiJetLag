Privacy Policy for Anti Jet Lag

Last updated: November 17, 2025

Simple Summary: Anti Jet Lag does not collect, store, or share any of your personal information. Everything you enter stays on your device only.

1. Information Collection

Anti Jet Lag does not collect any personal information from users. We do not:

  • Collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Track your location or travel history
  • Store your sleep schedules on our servers
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other device data

2. Data Storage

All data you enter into Anti Jet Lag (such as timezone selections, sleep schedules, and flight dates) is stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Is not backed up to cloud services by our app
  • Remains private to you

3. Permissions

Anti Jet Lag requests the following permissions:

  • Alarm Permission: To allow you to export wake-up times to your device's Clock app
  • Location Permission (Optional): Only used to auto-detect your current timezone for convenience. Your actual location is never stored or transmitted.

You can use the app without granting location permission by manually selecting your timezone.

4. Third-Party Services

Anti Jet Lag does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not:

  • Display advertisements
  • Use Google Analytics or similar services
  • Share data with third parties
  • Include social media tracking

5. Internet Connection

Anti Jet Lag works completely offline. The app does not require an internet connection to function, and no data is transmitted over the internet.

6. Children's Privacy

Anti Jet Lag is designed for adults 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

7. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security settings. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device locked with a password or biometric security
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using device encryption if available

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the Anti Jet Lag app, or
  • Clearing the app's data in your device settings

This will permanently remove all schedules and preferences from your device.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or Anti Jet Lag, please contact us at:

Email: antijetlag@example.com

11. Your Rights

Since Anti Jet Lag does not collect any personal data, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over all app data through your device settings.

Summary: Your privacy is important to us. Anti Jet Lag is designed to be completely private - we don't collect, store, or share any of your information. Everything stays on your device, under your control.
